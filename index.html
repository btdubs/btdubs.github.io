Samples of My Work

Settlers of Catan 3D Printed Magnetic Board

A custom 3D modelled board for the game Settlers of Catan. The pieces were 3D printed, and I hand painted them. Each piece has magnets on the side to allow them to interlock and connect. They are now available for sale at my online shop.

Georgia Tech RoboJackets RoboRacing

I work on the software team for the competition GT Robotics team, RoboRacing. We build autonomous small-scale race cars that use ROS, OpenCV, and an array of sensors to navigate circuit and obstacle courses. We currently have two cars, a hobby-sized rc car named Sedani and a go-kart sized car named Bigoli. I work on everything from computer vision to find road lines and signs to firmware that ensures the car moves.

Check out our RoboJackets GitHub to learn more. https://github.com/RoboJackets/roboracing-software

FIRST Competition Robots

These are 3 years worth of competition robots I programmed and designed during my time in high school. These robots have all competed in the FIRST Georgia State Championship and one even won! Scissor lifts, linear screw drives, multi-leveled pulley systems, omni-wheels and many other mechanical designs are used by these robots. They are programmed in C and Java and have won awards for autonomous path movements and design.

I now mentor a other robotics teams and help teach middle and high school students about robotics, programming, and engineering.

3D Modelling Art

These are a subset of art I have made using 3D and 2D software. Most are made in Blender3D, Inkscape, and GIMP. My 3D art focuses not only on the art and composition aspect, but also the technical aspect of good topology.
