My Work

Competition Robot

FIRST Competition Robot I programmed and designed. This robot won the FIRST Georgia State Competition. The design expands from a small eighteen inch height to over five feet by utilizing a scissor lift mechanism. Based off a LEGO NXT brick, this robot is programmed in C and won awards for its autonomous path movements.

Settlers of Catan Magnetic Board

3D modeled Settlers of Catan board. These pieces were then 3D printed and magnets placed in the sides to allow them to interlock and connect.

3D Guitar Studio Rendering

A 3D model designed in Blender 3D and rendering using Cycles Rendering Engine. Modeled with respect to good topology and quad faces. The material textures are custom procedurally generated and put into studio lighting.
